# Photos and Videos

OpenCloud can preview photos and play videos directly in the web interface.
This makes it easier to review media without downloading files first.

## Photo preview

Open a photo in the preview view to browse through the photo roll.

<img src={require("./img/photos-and-videos/photo-roll-screenshot.png").default} alt="Photo roll view in OpenCloud" width="1920"/>

The photo viewer supports photo roll style navigation, so you can move through a series of images without leaving the preview.

## Video playback

Videos open in the same preview area and can be played directly in the browser.

<img src={require("./img/photos-and-videos/video-screenshot.png").default} alt="Video preview in OpenCloud" width="1920"/>

Video playback works directly in OpenCloud, so you can review clips without downloading them first.

## Notes

- The experience depends on the file type and browser capabilities.
- Very large media files may still take a moment to load.
- This feature is meant for quick viewing, not for advanced media editing.
