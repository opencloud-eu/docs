# Theme

The **Theme** setting allows you to customize the appearance of the OpenCloud app.

You can choose whether the app follows your device's appearance or always uses a light or dark theme.

## Change the Theme

1. Open the **OpenCloud** app.
2. Tap **Settings**.
3. Under **User Interface**, tap **Theme**.

The **Theme** page allows you to select how the OpenCloud app is displayed.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/theme/theme-button.png").default}
alt="Theme Button"
height="650"/>

## Available Themes

You can choose one of the following appearance options:

- **System Appearance** – Uses your iOS appearance setting and automatically switches between Light and Dark mode.
- **Light** – Always displays the app using the light theme.
- **Dark** – Always displays the app using the dark theme.

Select the appearance that best matches your preference.

The selected theme is applied immediately throughout the app.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/theme/theme-menu.png").default}
alt="Theme Menu"
height="650"/>

## How Theme Selection Works

Changes take effect immediately after selecting a theme.

If **System Appearance** is selected, OpenCloud automatically follows the current iOS appearance and switches between Light and Dark mode whenever your device theme changes.

:::tip

Using **System Appearance** is recommended, as it provides the most consistent user experience by matching your device's appearance settings.

:::
