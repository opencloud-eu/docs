# Shares

The OpenCloud Android App allows you to securely share files and folders with other users, groups, or anyone who has a public link. This section explains the available sharing methods and shows you how to configure permissions and share content directly from your Android device.

## In this section

- [Invite to Share](./invite.md)  
  Share files and folders with users and groups within your OpenCloud instance and configure their access permissions.

- [Share via Link](./links.md)  
  Create password-protected public links, configure link settings such as expiration dates, and securely share files and folders with others.
