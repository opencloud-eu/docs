# Terms Of Use

The **Terms Of Use** section provides quick access to the OpenCloud Terms of Use in your device's default web browser.

## Open the Terms Of Use

1. Open the **OpenCloud** app.
2. Tap **Settings**.
3. Under **More**, tap **Terms Of Use**.

Selecting this option opens a confirmation dialog before leaving the app.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/terms-of-use/terms of use button.png").default}
alt="Terms Of Use Button" height="650"/>

## Confirm Opening the Website

A confirmation dialog appears asking whether you want to open the external website.

- Tap **OK** to continue.
- Tap **Cancel** to remain in the OpenCloud app.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/terms-of-use/pop up.png").default}
alt="Pop Up" height="650"/>

## View the Terms Of Use

After confirming, OpenCloud opens the Terms of Use in your device's default web browser.

The page contains the complete license and terms governing the use of OpenCloud software and related components.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/terms-of-use/terms of use page.png").default}
alt="OpenCloud Terms Of Use website" height="650"/>

:::tip

An internet connection is required to access the Terms Of Use because the content is loaded from the official OpenCloud website.

:::

:::note

Opening the Terms Of Use launches your device's default web browser. You can return to the OpenCloud app at any time.

:::
