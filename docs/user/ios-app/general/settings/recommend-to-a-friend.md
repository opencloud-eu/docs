# Recommend to a Friend

The **Recommend to a Friend** feature allows you to quickly invite others to try OpenCloud. The app automatically creates a pre-filled invitation message containing a download link that you can share using your preferred communication app.

## Share an Invitation

1. Open the **OpenCloud** app.
2. Tap **Settings**.
3. Under **More**, tap **Recommend to a friend**.

Selecting this option opens your device's share sheet with a pre-filled invitation message.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/recommend-to-a-friend/recommend to a friend button.png").default}
alt="Recommend to a Friend in Settings" height="650"/>

## Send the Invitation

Choose the app you want to use for sharing, such as **Mail**, **Messages**, or another supported sharing service.

The invitation message already contains:

- A pre-filled subject.
- A short invitation message.
- A download link to OpenCloud.

Simply enter the recipient and send the message.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/recommend-to-a-friend/message field.png").default}
alt="Share invitation" height="650"/>

:::tip

You can share the invitation using any app that supports the iOS Share Sheet, including Mail, Messages, WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram, and many others.

:::

:::note

The generated download link always points to the latest official version of the OpenCloud mobile app available for the recipient's device.

:::
