# Privacy Policy

The **Privacy Policy** section provides quick access to OpenCloud's official privacy policy in your device's default web browser.

## Open the Privacy Policy

1. Open the **OpenCloud** app.
2. Tap **Settings**.
3. Under **More**, tap **Privacy Policy**.

Selecting this option opens a confirmation dialog before leaving the app.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/privacy-policy/privacy policy button.png").default}
alt="Privacy Policy Button" height="650"/>

## Confirm Opening the Website

A confirmation dialog appears asking whether you want to open the external website.

- Tap **OK** to continue.
- Tap **Cancel** to remain in the OpenCloud app.

<img src={require("../../img/settings/privacy-policy/pop up.png").default}
alt="Pop up" height="650"/>

## View the Privacy Policy

After confirming, OpenCloud opens the official Privacy Policy in your device's default web browser.

The page contains information about:

- Data processing
- Privacy practices
- GDPR compliance
- Your rights regarding personal data

<img src={require("../../img/settings/privacy-policy/privacy policy page.png").default}
alt="OpenCloud Privacy Policy website" height="650"/>

:::tip

An internet connection is required to access the Privacy Policy because it is loaded from the official OpenCloud website.

:::

:::note

Opening the Privacy Policy launches your device's default web browser. You can return to the OpenCloud app at any time.

:::
